The Akshaya Patra Foundation

The Akshaya Patra foundation is not-for-profit organization headquartered in Bengaluru , India.The Foundation strives to eliminate classroom hunger by implementing the PM POSHAN(Mid -Day Meal)Programme. It provides nutiritious meals to children studying in government school and government schoold and government aided schools.
